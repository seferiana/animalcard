Blue Glaucus

Also known as the blue dragon, sea swallow, or blue angel, the blue glaucus is a species of brightly colored sea slug.

  • Scientific Name: Glaucus atlanticus
  • Length: Three centimeters
  • Ecosystem: Open Ocean
  • Region: Atlantic, Pacific, and Indian Oceans

The Blue Glaucus lives throughout the open ocean. It often floats on its backside, showing its brightly colored underbelly to airborne predators.
